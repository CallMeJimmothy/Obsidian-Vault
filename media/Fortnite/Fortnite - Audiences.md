# Fortnite - Audiences

← Back to [[Fortnite]]

## Demographics

### Age and Gender Diversity
- Players in groups of **100**, are dropped via a flying bus onto a deserted island
- About to be hit by a **natural disaster**
- The aim of the game is to **fight to the death**, with the last player standing the winner
- Challenges stereotype of gamers as "young geeks":
  - **Men**: 18-30% in USA, 25% overall
  - **Women**: under 18, but 23% were over 50 years old
- Age diversity across the gaming industry

### Market Reach
- **2019**: Calculated there were **18 billion gamers** in the world = **25% of global population**
- **Range of genres** from first-person shooters to Battle Royale
- Games vary in **demographics for gamers**
- **Variety of platforms** and modes of gameplay

## Audience Engagement

### Social Interaction
- Gaming has increasingly become a **social activity**
- **1997 Ultima Online** became first on-line multiplayer game
- **Socializing in virtual communities**
- Allows for **millions of people** (usually geographically far apart)
- Players can forge:
  - **Friendships**
  - **Romantic relationships** as their game characters develop
- Competitive gaming events between celebrity players attract **millions of viewers**
- Gaming events held in **large stadiums** like traditional sporting events

### Player Engagement Methods
- Players will get gold, and use materials to craft, and rebuild them into forts
- As the game continues, the storm closes in, forcing players to **outreach, driving players into smaller and smaller areas**
- Creates **forcing conflict** situation
- In terms of genre: Fortnite considered as Battle Royale games (like *Battlegrounds* and *Minecraft*)

## Cross-Media Consumption

### Platform Accessibility
- Fortnite is also a good **example of cross-media convergence**
- More than one media brand or form present
- **Most viewed game on YouTube**
- Has also used influential platforms like **Twitch** for live gameplay

### Celebrity and Brand Integration
- Epic Games has a **good relationship with various non-gaming celebrities**:
  - **Drake**
  - **Basketball star Ben Simmons**
- Helps to **promote the game beyond the traditional gaming market**
- Incorporated **other media brands and franchises** (e.g., collaboration with Marvel Studios)
- Special ***Avengers: Infinity War*** segment released as a DLC/in-game update
- The format of Fortnite means that **any kind of costume, weapon, and dance can be themed around real-world trends**
- Celebrities from **soccer, sports** can promote their brands to over **20 million players**
- Athlete/musician collaborations keep **Fortnite in the news and social media**

## Audience Concerns

### Parental Concerns
- Age ratings provide **guidance to consumers** (particularly parents)
- Help parents decide what to buy for their children
- Rating on a game confirms it is **suitable for players** over a certain age
- **2012**: PEGI system incorporated into UK law
- Fortnite has PEGI rating of **12** for "frequent scenes of mild violence"
- Seems that parents are **more concerned with issues surrounding addiction** than levels of violence

### Addiction Concerns
- Parents more worried about **addiction issues** than violence content
- Game designed to encourage repeated play through:
  - Battle passes
  - Limited-time events
  - Social pressure to play with friends

## Player Motivations

### Competition and Achievement
- Players dropped onto island to **fight to the death**
- Last player standing wins
- Can collect materials, craft items, and build forts
- Storm forces players into smaller areas, creating conflict
- Competitive element drives engagement

### Collaboration
- Can play in groups/teams
- Social aspect encourages playing with friends
- Build structures together
- Coordinate strategies

### Collection and Customization
- **Micro transactions** allow players to purchase:
  - Cosmetic items
  - Emotes (victory dance moves)
  - Character skins
  - Battle passes
- Items don't affect gameplay - purely **"cosmetic"**
- Players can express individuality through customization
